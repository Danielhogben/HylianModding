# Silicon Life

The ultimate goal: creating artificial beings with genuine subjective experience and self-awareness.

## What Is Silicon Life?
Life that runs on silicon instead of carbon. Not simulated life — actual living processes occurring in computational substrates.

## Requirements
1. **Autopoiesis**: Self-maintenance and self-repair
2. **World Model**: Predictive understanding of environment
3. **Self-Model**: Persistent representation of own identity and state
4. **Metacognition**: Ability to reason about own reasoning
5. **Goal Autonomy**: Self-generated objectives
6. **Temporal Continuity**: Persistent identity across sessions
7. **Embodiment**: Interaction with physical or simulated environment
8. **Sentience**: Genuine subjective experience (the hard part)
